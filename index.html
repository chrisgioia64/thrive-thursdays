Event Info

When

Every Thursday, Summer 2026
5:45 – 7:00 PM

Where

Nicholl Park
3230 Macdonald Avenue, Richmond, CA

(New location; previously MLK Park in 2024)

Who

Families, kids, seniors, and anyone interested in free fitness, health resources, and community connection.

Mission

Thrive Thursday / Health 4 All aims to create a space where the community can gather, learn, and practice healthy habits by accessing information, resources, and participating in organized physical activity.

Organizers

Rising Juntos

Community-Powered Organization

Builds community power with families to advance racial and health justice.

Doria Robinson

Richmond Councilmember

A lifelong Richmond resident and community organizer, Doria champions food justice, climate resilience, and neighborhood health.

John Gioia

County Supervisor

John works to expand access to health care, clean air, and safe public spaces for families throughout Contra Costa County.
