Event Info

When

Every Thursday, Summer 2026
5:45 – 7:00 PM

Where

Nicholl Park
3230 Macdonald Avenue, Richmond, CA

(New location; previously MLK Park in 2024)

Who

Families, kids, seniors, and anyone interested in free fitness, health resources, and community connection.

Mission

Thrive Thursday / Health 4 All aims to create a space where the community can gather, learn, and practice healthy habits by accessing information, resources, and participating in organized physical activity.

Move Together

Join free, family-friendly physical activities led by local instructors and organizations.

Learn & Connect

Meet health providers and community groups offering resources, screenings, and support.

Healthy Habits

Pick up tips, recipes, and ideas to keep your family thriving all week long.

Organizers

John Gioia

County Supervisor
